荣誉里程 Highlights

从少年天赋到国际大奖，她的每一步都引向更广阔的舞台。 From early prodigy to international laureate, each step opened a wider stage.

耀眼启航 Brilliant Launch

6岁开始学琴，获香港音乐公开赛少年组金奖、斯坦威钢琴比赛中国总赛区冠军。10岁以第一名考入中央音乐学院附小六年级，后保送附中。 Begins piano at six; wins the Hong Kong Music Open Competition (Youth Gold) and the Steinway Piano Competition China Finals. At ten, enters the Central Conservatory of Music Primary School (Grade 6) in first place, later promoted to the middle school.

国际初绽 International Emergence

12岁在罗马尼亚Competition Jeunesses Musicales Bucharest越级夺冠，赴美国吉娜.巴考厄国际钢琴比赛获Junior组亚军，日本亚洲青年肖邦比赛获银奖。 At twelve, wins the Competition Jeunesses Musicales Bucharest in the 14-18 age group; earns Junior Second Prize at the Gina Bachauer International Piano Competition; wins Silver at the Japan Asia Youth Chopin Competition.

世界舞台 World Stage

13岁赴美首演，在迈阿密国际钢琴音乐节与乐队演奏《肖邦第一钢琴协奏曲》，被柯蒂斯音乐学院录取，并获茱莉亚预科全额奖学金。 At thirteen, makes her U.S. debut at the Miami International Piano Festival performing Chopin Piano Concerto No. 1, later admitted to the Curtis Institute of Music and awarded full scholarship to Juilliard Pre-College.

持续绽放 Continuing Ascent

2007阿斯本国际音乐节比赛第一名，2008美国吉娜.巴考厄国际钢琴比赛青年艺术家组金奖，成为邓泰山首位私人学生。 First Prize at the 2007 Aspen Music Festival Piano Concerto Competition; Gold Prize at the 2008 Gina Bachauer International Piano Competition (Young Artist Division); becomes Dang Thai Son's first private student.

艺术旅程 Artistic Journey

从国内巡演到世界名校，艺术道路清晰而坚定。 From national tours to the world's leading conservatories, her path is clear and steadfast.

6-11岁 Ages 6-11

耀眼启航 Radiant Start

6岁开始学琴，获香港音乐公开赛少年组金奖、斯坦威钢琴比赛中国总赛区冠军。10岁赴北京师从鲍蕙荞，以第一名考入中央音乐学院附小六年级，后直接保送附中。11岁在佛山金马音乐厅举办个人独奏音乐会，并与厦门爱乐合作《贝多芬第一钢琴协奏曲》。 Started piano at six; won the Hong Kong Music Open Competition Youth Gold and the Steinway Piano Competition China Finals. At ten, moved to Beijing to study with Bao Huiqiao, entered the Central Conservatory of Music Primary School (Grade 6) in first place, later promoted to the middle school. At eleven, gave a solo recital at Foshan Jinma Concert Hall and performed Beethoven Piano Concerto No. 1 with Xiamen Philharmonic.

12岁 Age 12

华彩绽放 Colorful Bloom

在罗马尼亚Competition Jeunesses Musicales Bucharest越级参加14-18岁组并获冠军，同年获美国吉娜.巴考厄国际钢琴比赛Junior组亚军，并在日本亚洲青年肖邦比赛获银奖。 Won first prize at Competition Jeunesses Musicales Bucharest, competing above age in the 14-18 group. The same year, earned Junior Second Prize at the Gina Bachauer International Piano Competition and Silver at the Japan Asia Youth Chopin Competition.

13岁 Age 13

美国首演 U.S. Debut

在迈阿密国际钢琴音乐节与迈阿密乐队演奏《肖邦第一钢琴协奏曲》，并举办多场独奏音乐会。被柯蒂斯音乐学院录取为Gary Graffman与Claude Frank学生，同时获茱莉亚预科全额奖学金、斯坦威钢琴赞助与PCS全额学费支持，师从Yoheved Kaplinsky。 Performed Chopin Piano Concerto No. 1 with the Miami orchestra at the Miami International Piano Festival and presented multiple solo recitals. Admitted to the Curtis Institute of Music as a student of Gary Graffman and Claude Frank, while also receiving a full scholarship to Juilliard Pre-College, Steinway sponsorship, and full tuition at PCS (Professional Children School), studying with Yoheved Kaplinsky.

14-16岁 Ages 14-16

高光突破 Breakthrough

2007阿斯本国际音乐节钢琴协奏曲比赛第一名，成为最年轻获奖者，与American Academy of Conducting at Aspen Orchestra在Aspen Benedict Music Tent演出并获大伟.兹曼高度评价。2008获美国吉娜.巴考厄国际钢琴比赛青年艺术家组金奖，受邀全美巡演，成为邓泰山首位私人学生。 Won First Prize at the 2007 Aspen Music Festival Piano Concerto Competition, becoming its youngest winner, and performed with the American Academy of Conducting at Aspen Orchestra at Aspen Benedict Music Tent, praised by conductor David Zinman. In 2008 she won the Gold Prize at the Gina Bachauer International Piano Competition (Young Artist Division), toured the U.S., and became Dang Thai Son's first private student.

17岁起 Age 17+

名校深造 Advanced Studies

2009获加拿大皇家音乐学院校长邀请免试并全额奖学金入学，获Dean's List奖，师从John Perry并取得最高演奏博士文凭。后受邀赴瑞士巴塞尔音乐学院就读最高演奏文凭，师从Filippo Gamba与Claudio Martinez Mehner。 In 2009, invited by the President of The Royal Conservatory of Music in Canada and admitted with full scholarship. Earned Dean's List honors, studied with John Perry, and completed the highest performance doctoral diploma. Later invited to pursue the highest performance diploma at Musik Akademie Basel, studying with Filippo Gamba and Claudio Martinez Mehner.

艺术家之路 Artist's Path

舞台、教育与音乐组织并行，她的影响力在全球持续延展。 Stage, education, and music initiatives evolve in parallel, expanding her global influence.

艺术家之路 On Stage

自2006年起受邀在迈阿密举办多场独奏音乐会，并与乐队合作协奏曲音乐会，后受聘为迈阿密国际钢琴音乐节常任艺术家。2013应作曲家德沃夏克曾侄孙John Dvorak邀请参赛Marta Hidy协奏曲大赛并获冠军，与多伦多交响乐团首演。2015起担任迈阿密国际钢琴音乐节大师班北美及亚洲区首席代表，2017录制首张肖邦专辑。 Since 2006, invited to present solo recitals in Miami and perform concertos with orchestra, later appointed resident artist of the Miami International Piano Festival. In 2013, invited by composer John Dvorak (grandnephew of Dvorak) to compete in the Marta Hidy Concerto Competition, winning first prize and premiering with the Toronto Symphony Orchestra. From 2015, served as North America and Asia representative for festival masterclasses, and recorded her first Chopin album in 2017.

作曲与指挥 Composition & Conducting

除钢琴演奏外，斯婧的作曲与指挥亦备受肯定。指挥受到多伦多爱乐前首席指挥Uri Mayer高度评价并破例收为私人学生，多次获瑞士、法国指挥家Rodolfo-Fischer赞扬。 Beyond piano performance, her composition and conducting are widely praised. Her conducting was highly acclaimed by Uri Mayer, former principal conductor of the Toronto Philharmonic, who accepted her as a private student. She has also received acclaim from Swiss and French conductor Rodolfo Fischer.

教学与机构 Education & Institutions

2016创办贝丽斯慕音乐艺术学校BAMA及BAMA国际音乐节，任艺术总监。BAMA国际音乐节将迎来十周年庆典，将在纽约卡内基音乐厅与法国巴黎Kawaii音乐厅举办大师班与音乐会庆祝。BAMA在2024年于深圳开办分校，目前学员超300名，多名学生获全额奖学金进入欧美及中国著名音乐学院。斯婧已连续5年被北美斯坦威评为Top Music Teacher，2025受邀担任长江学院派艺术家。 In 2016, founded the BAMA Music Arts School and the BAMA International Music Festival, serving as Artistic Director. The festival will celebrate its 10th anniversary with masterclasses and concerts at Carnegie Hall in New York and Kawai Hall in Paris. BAMA opened a Shenzhen campus in 2024 with over 300 students; many win international prizes and full scholarships to leading conservatories in Europe, North America, and China. She has been named a North American Steinway Top Music Teacher for five consecutive years and was invited as a Changjiang Academy arts faculty artist in 2025.

赛事与评审 Competitions & Jury

2020发起并联合创办Aegio国际钢琴比赛，担任音乐总监。赛事获阿林克-阿格里奇基金会认证并成为成员，目前在北美、欧洲和澳洲设有15个分赛区。她多次担任国际比赛评委与评委主席，包括莫斯科萧邦青少年国际钢琴比赛总决赛、多伦多协奏曲大赛、奥地利Elevato国际钢琴比赛、香港青少年国际钢琴比赛、波兰IOPC-阿图尔.鲁宾斯坦钢琴奖、日本名古屋国际青少年钢琴比赛、李斯特青少年国际钢琴大赛、加拿大AEGIO国际钢琴大赛等。 In 2020, she co-founded the Aegio International Piano Competition and serves as Music Director. The competition is accredited by the Alink-Argerich Foundation and has 15 regional divisions across North America, Europe, and Australia. She frequently serves as juror and jury chair for major competitions, including the Moscow Chopin International Youth Piano Competition Finals, Orchestra Toronto Concerto Competition, Austria Elevato International Piano Competition, Hong Kong International Youth Piano Competition, Poland IOPC Artur Rubinstein Piano Prize, Nagoya International Youth Piano Competition, Liszt International Youth Piano Competition, and the AEGIO International Piano Competition.
